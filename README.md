## Summary

A cross-substrate structural invariant — seven components governed by a quadratic constraint with bifurcation producing a third element — is instantiated across five physically distinct substrates: electrochemical memristor, grezistor (predicted), NV-center qubit, discrete time crystal, and FM/NM/FM spintronics (GMR/TMR/STO). Three discrete invariants (residue channel count, autonomy sign, bifurcation class) transfer across substrates sharing a vacancy topology and are individually falsifiable.

**10 Tier 1 predictions.** Four testable with existing equipment. Three require constructing new devices. Three require specialized apparatus. None contradicts known physics.
